About the Game

Our game is designed to challenge your knowledge and skills through various levels of increasing difficulty. Each level presents a unique set of questions and tasks that you need to complete to advance. The game features dynamic question generation, random element placement, and a variety of interaction types to keep you engaged.

How to Play

  1. Enter your name to start the game.
  2. Complete each level by answering the questions correctly.
  3. You can exit the level at any time, but you must complete the current level to advance to the next one.
  4. Your score will be calculated based on your performance, and you can see your ranking on the rating page.
  5. You can restart the game at any time, possibly with a new name.
